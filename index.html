About Me

I am a Senior Data Engineer with 8+ years building the backbone of data-driven organisations. I have shipped systems that process trillions of records, led migrations that halved infrastructure costs, and built platforms that let the leadership make better decisions.

My work lives at the intersection of software engineering discipline and the messy realities of real-world data — late events, schema drift, upstream incidents, and the stakeholder who needs a new dashboard by tomorrow. I care about readable, clear, and maintainable code.
